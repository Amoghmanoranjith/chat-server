Samsung Research: AI Business Cases

Samsung Gauss: Proprietary Generative AI

  • Gauss Language: Large language model for intelligent text processing.
  • Gauss Code: AI-powered software development assistant.
  • Gauss Image: Capable of generating and editing high-quality images.
  • Planned integration across devices for enhanced user experience and productivity.

On-Device AI

  • Running AI models locally on hardware for speed and privacy.
  • Optimized for energy efficiency using custom hardware (NPUs).
  • Enables AI applications without relying on cloud infrastructure.

Natural Language Processing

  • Enhancing Bixby with improved contextual understanding and dialogue flow.
  • Real-time conversation, emotional tone detection, and personalized interactions.

Multimodal Interaction

  • Combines text, image, voice, and context for more intuitive AI interfaces.
  • Advances in vision-language models and gesture recognition.

AI for Health and Wellness

  • Wearables and smart devices using AI for real-time health insights.
  • Predictive diagnostics, fitness coaching, and sleep analysis.

Federated Learning & Edge AI

  • Privacy-preserving AI through decentralized training.
  • Collaborative model improvements across multiple devices without sharing raw data.

Strategic Goals

  • Full integration of AI across all Samsung consumer products.
  • AI-first strategy to compete with both hardware and software giants.
  • Global AI research expansion via partnerships with academia and startups.
  • Co-design of AI software and custom hardware for peak performance.
